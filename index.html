About Us

It’s hard to describe the Wave in a nutshell. It’s probably because the Wave is not just about one thing -- we have several functions and interests, and it’s all in one giant salad bowl. What we mean to say is, the Wave has multiple verticals, but none of its members is divided under them. We prefer to allow every member to engage in every conversation and event we carry out. The wave is something that can truly be understood by being a part of it. However, we’ll try our best to explain it to you!

THE WAVE

The Wave is a student organisation made for and by students of GITAM School of Humanities and Social Sciences. We focus on the humanitarian aspect of everything: society, culture, art, media, education, and more. We are a safe space for free thought and expression. We encourage our fellow students to think critically, analyse everything, and recognise the perspective of not only themselves but the people around them. The Wave Forums were born for this purpose. As a platform, we also give space for artists to express their passions, which is why our blog is so important to us. We showcase the reflective work of all our members every week on it.

Why GSHS in particular?

Well, we aren’t a closed community: any GITAM Hyderabad student may join us. However, we put a specific emphasis on GSHS because home is where the heart is, right? Our original driving idea was to bring all GSHS students together.

What does The Wave do?

Besides providing a platform for the creators of GITAM to grow, the Wave is a place for mutual learning and essential conversation about society, culture, art, media, and education. It is also a community GSHS students can fall back on for support in their endeavours.

Why “The Wave”?

A wave is one of the most potent forces of nature. Waves keep rolling; it is up to us to catch and ride one. The Wave stands for all the opportunities it offers to the students of GSHS and GITAM at large to learn, grow, and create.

Cool! How can I join?

We love the enthusiasm! If you’re a GITAM Hyderabad student, you can join us by directly submitting your statement of purpose with a few details about you at or, you can send us your artwork (music, writing, photography, etc.) at Wave.gitam@gmail.com to reach the GITAM audience!
